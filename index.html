Salt Lake Temple

The Salt Lake Temple is one of the most recognizable of all the temples of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Days Saints. It took 40 years to build and was dedicated in 1893. It is located in Salt Lake City Utah near the headquarters of the Church. It is also the largest temple per floor area. People visit the grounds of the Salt Lake Temple even if they do not enter. The grounds are especially busy during the Christmas holiday season because of the Christmas lights that are displayed in great number.
